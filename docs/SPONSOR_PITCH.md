## The opportunity

Wausau Pilot & Review is where locals look first for news — and Wisconsin sports is one of the
strongest traffic magnets we have. The **Brewers Tracker** is our flagship: a live, single-scroll
stats experience (season pulse, NL Central race, schedule, team leaders) that updates itself
directly from Major League Baseball's data, all day, every game day.

**Ho-Chunk Gaming Wittenberg is the founding title sponsor.** This one-pager is the case for
expanding that partnership across more of our sports coverage.

## Why it works for a sponsor

- **Dwell time, not a banner glance.** The widget is interactive and self-updating. During a live
  game the score, inning, bases, and count tick in real time — readers stay and watch, with your
  brand on the marquee the whole time.
- **Premium, native placement.** Not an ad slot bolted on — your logo is the title sponsor of the
  page, plus a credit on the live game hero. It reads as a partnership, not an interruption.
- **Built for your brand.** Tasteful, on-brand presentation including a responsible-gaming line —
  we understand the gaming category and present it the right way.
- **Provable ROI.** Cookieless analytics give a clean monthly report: impressions (page views) and
  click-throughs per placement. Your link carries campaign tags so the traffic shows up in your
  own analytics, too.
